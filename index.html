About the Competition

Dear Participants,

Multi-leaf rubble-stone masonry buildings are highly vulnerable masonry typologies. Their structural response depends on both the mechanical properties of stone and mortar, and the microstructure, defined by the size, shape, and arrangement of stones. This competition provides experimental data and digital twins for three selected walls, inviting participants to submit blind predictions of their monotonic in-plane shear-compression responses before testing at the Structural Engineering Platform (GIS), EPFL, Lausanne, Switzerland.
